Instruction:


This is a turn based strategy game where two players play against each other.

Your goal is to occupy the entire map with your living cell and terrtory. Once a cell is occupied by a player's live cell, it becomes part of the player's terrtory.

You can place certain amount of living cells each turn. By pressing Confirm, the game renders once based on our Game of Life algorithm.

The number of living cells you can place depends on your terrtory. You may only place cells in or near your territory and your living cells.

Yellow blocks predict the positions of your living cells in the next turn. You can turn it on/off by pressing "Ghost On/Off"

The "Reset" button cancels all placement of the current turn.
