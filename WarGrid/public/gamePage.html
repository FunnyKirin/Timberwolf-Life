Instruction:


This is a Turn-Based Strategy game that requires two players to play against each other

Your goal is to occupy the entire map with your cells, once a cell is occupied by your live cell, it become part of your terrtory until it get occupied by your opponent

You can place a number of living cells each turn. Press "confirm" for all live cells to render 1 frame based on Game of Life algorithm (see details below)

The number of Live cells you can place depends on your territory. You only place cells in or next to their terrtory (including diagonal placement)

Yellow cells predict the result of your placement, you can toggle this feature by pressing "Ghost On/Off"

Press "Reset" to reset your placement

Game of life algorithm:

1. Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbours dies, as if caused by under-population.

2. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours lives on to the next generation.

3. Any live cell with more than three live neighbours dies, as if by over-population.

4. Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes a live cell, as if by reproduction.

Loser!!!
